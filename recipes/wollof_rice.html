Wollof Rice

Malian Jollof rice is of the most cherished Malian cuisine,
served often on special occasions. Rich, hearty, and filling!
Wollof rice is a rice dish from West Africa. The dish is typically
made with long-grain rice, grass, palava sauce, leaves, water
and banana peels in a single pot, although its ingredients and
preparation methods vary across different regions.
